Yoga classes

The practice of yoga is about bringing an internal connection and deeper awareness, creating space and opening up to a stronger and more flexible body as we beging to balance the body's energy and physicality.

Students will learnhow to the correct use of the arms and legs in the postures (asnas) open the spine. Standing postures are the pwoerful grounding physical foundation for the overall ractice that prepares us for the floor-based postures.

The use of blocks, straps and bricks can help bring more understanding of alignment in the postures, allowing the student to work in a deeper way, creating the best possible alignment whilst their bodies open up.

Classes are suitable for beginners as well as those with a more estabished regular practice.
